About WordCamp

The beautiful city of Prague will be the host city for WordCamp!

Explore the future of WordPress with a lineup of industry professionals. Discover new techniques to advance your career as a web designer.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Want to meet the international WordPress community and share skills with some of the world's top experts and makers? Be the first to know what to expect for the future of WordPress.

WordCamp is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

What You'll Learn

WordPress 5: New Gutenberg editor WooCommerce: E-commerce platform Hosting: Managed web hosting Themes: Creating your theme Plugins: Develop your plugin Content: Writing and podcasts

Speakers

Mr. Ted E. Bear

Mr. Lin Ux

Mr. Lin Ux comes from a very far away part of Antiarctica. He is looking forward to teaching a class on Linux operations and how you can add Linux to your life!

Ms. Uni Corn

Ms. Uni Corn is here from Lyon, France and she is very excited to talk about the new advances in the Adobe Color spectrum.

Mr. Buster O. Criminals

Mr. Buster O. Criminals comes from America. He is a very young but experienced boy who is eager to help with any of your IT security questions and to talk about new advances in security.

Mr. Ramold Hardware

Mr. Ramold Hardware is from Scotland. He is pleased to be introducing the newest RAM upgrades that are available to all of those in the coding and design fields. Upgrading hardware is what he lives for!

Mr. Peter Python

Mr. Peter Python is joining us from India. He is here to speak with you about the advances in the Python programming language and how to you can switch over to Python in your line of work.
